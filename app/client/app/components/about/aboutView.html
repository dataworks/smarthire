About Us

Our Goal

At the start of the nine week internship program at Data Works, we were presented with a real life situation that we would solve by building a web application. Every year, Data Works receives hundreds of resumes for both the internship program and full time positions. Having to go through each individual resume to find the most qualified candidiates can be very tedious and time-consuming.

ResCheck is a web application reads in an uploaded resume and assigns a score based on the applicant's qualifications. After reviewing the applicant fully, an employer can then decide whether the applicant is a good fit for the company or not. We hope that this product will help an employer save time in the screening resumes and that it can improve the hiring process.
